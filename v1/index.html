MATHÍAS FONDO

Photo

Mathías Fondo is a PhD Student in Economics at the Department of Economics of the University of Bologna, Italy.

His research interests lie at the intersection of applied microeconomics, public economics, and labor economics. He is an applied researcher whose work focuses on labor market dynamics, the effects of taxation, and income inequality.

Prior to joining the University of Bologna, he worked as a Research Assistant at the Department of Economics, Universidad de la República (Uruguay) and as a Consultant at CPA Ferrere.
